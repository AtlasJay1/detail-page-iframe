About the loan
Parichat is requesting TB67988.82 ($1600) for a new cutting table, overlocker, workplace training for her new staff and to implement a workplace health and safety plan for her business.
About the location
Chaing Mai
Chaing Mai is located in the northern region of Thailand. It is well-known for its arts and crafts industries.

Parichat is an entrepreneur who has been working as a seamstress for 18 years. She started her small alterations and repairs business in a space in her living room where she carefully attends to her clients’ needs with quality service. She has taught one of her daughters the art of sewing, and they both support each other in the business. Their business hours are from 09:00 am to 7:00 pm every day.

Parichat's main clients are her neighbours, family members and the general public. Parichat’s average daily sales are TB1500.00 ($100.50) with a profit margin of 20% of her total income. Thanks to her effort and dedication, she has been able to support her husband with their household expenses.

What is microfinance?
Many of Lendwithcare's local development partners are specialist microfinance institutions (MFIs). Microfinance refers to financial services for poor and low-income people. Although the main focus is on the provision of small loans, microfinance also includes the provision of other basic financial services such as savings, money transfer and insurance.
